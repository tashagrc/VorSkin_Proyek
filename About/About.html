ABOUT US

HISTORY

Vorskin was founded in 1998 in Indonesia and was founded by Natasha Grace Radika and Clarien Cahyono. Vorskin was founded to create skin-care and body-care products without hurting animals.

OUR MISSION

Vorskin's mission is to give a Skin-care and Body-care product that are vegan based, cruelty free and used non-toxic ingridients to nourish your skin from inside and outside.

 
 

VISION

Vorskin's Skin-care is composed of natural ingredients and are rich in vitamins. All products are guaranteed to be 100% cruelty-free because animals aren't harmed during the creation.
